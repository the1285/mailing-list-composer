This 1285 mailing list formatter supports certain markup features you may choose to use.
  • If a paragraph starts with -, it will be treated as a bulleted list item.
  • Numbered lists can be created by starting a line with either Arabic or Roman numerals followed immediately by a period or a right parenthesis. Examples include 17., XVII., or xvii. (Roman numerals must be either all uppercase or all lowercase; Xvii. will not work.) or 24), XXIV), or xxiv)
  • Standard email quotes can be created by starting paragraphs with a >. Remember that once formatting is applied to the mail, all lines of the paragraph will be prepended with the > character. The formatter is aware of this, and if you edit the paragraph and reformat it, everything will be taken care of by the formatter.
  • If you create a single line containing three or more dashes like --- or -------, it will expand to a full-size horizontal rule.
